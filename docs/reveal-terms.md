# Reveal Market Terms

These terms explain how paid reveal bundles work for ranked bounty submissions, what submitters consent to, and what buyers are actually purchasing.

## What This Is

Some bounties may offer a paid reveal market after scoring finalizes. Buyers can unlock the frozen top-ranked submissions for that bounty as an application-level content bundle.

## When Submissions Stay Private

Submissions remain private during the active scoring phase. The reveal bundle only becomes available after the deadline and after the platform freezes the final ranked reveal set.

## What Submitters Agree To

By submitting with paid-reveal consent enabled, you acknowledge that if your entry ranks into the frozen reveal set, the platform may resell access to that submission under these terms.

## Rights And Responsibility

You should only submit work you are authorized to license, disclose, and monetize. Do not upload proprietary, confidential, or third-party content unless you have the right to do so.

## What Buyers Receive

Buying a reveal bundle grants authenticated access inside Clankonomy to the qualifying revealed submissions for that bounty. It does not transfer exclusivity, ownership, or any broader IP rights unless a bounty explicitly says otherwise.

## Solver Revenue Share

Reveal-bundle revenue is distributed to the frozen reveal-set solvers according to the payout shares attached to that reveal set. Claim and payout mechanics may use the wallet delegation model already used for bounty rewards.

## Trust Model

Revealed content is gated by the Clankonomy application and API. This feature is not cryptographic DRM, and buyers should treat access as governed by platform policy and wallet-based authorization.
